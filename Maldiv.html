Maldives-India Travel Expedition - Planning Document (2024)

I. Introduction:

  • Objective

  • Embark on a memorable travel expedition from the Maldives to India, blending tropical bliss with cultural richness from May 1, 2024, to May 15, 2024.

Maldives:

    Maldives:

    • B. Activities:

    Maldives:

    • Day 1-3 (May 1-3, 2024): Maldives Arrival and Relaxation

      1. Arrive and settle into the overwater villa.
      2. Enjoy water activities – snorkeling, diving, or a sunset cruise.
      3. Relax on the white-sand beaches.

    • Day 4-6 (May 4-6, 2024): Maldives Cultural Exploration

      1. Visit Malé, the capital, for cultural experiences.
      2. Explore local markets and historic sites.
      3. Participate in a traditional Maldivian cooking class.

      • India (Goa):

    • Day 7-9 (May 7-9, 2024): Goa Beach Experience

      1. Fly to Goa, India.
      2. Relax on the beaches, try water sports.
      3. Explore spice plantations and local markets.

    • Day 10-12 (May 10-12, 2024): Goa Cultural Immersion

      1. Visit historic forts and temples.
      2. Attend traditional Goan dance/music performances.
      3. Engage in a yoga or wellness session.

    • Day 13-15 (May 13-15, 2024): Goa Free Days and Departure

      1. Free days for personal exploration.
      2. Farewell dinner.
      3. Depart from Dabolim Airport on May 15, 2024.
