    A. Day-to-Day Schedule:

    • 3. Days 9-14 (March 9-14, 2024): Everest Base Camp

  • Trek to Lobuche, Gorak Shep, and Everest Base Camp
  • Explore the Khumbu Icefall and surrounding areas
  • Acclimatization hikes and cultural interactions

    • Days 15-18 (March 15-18, 2024): Climbing Preparation and Ascent

  • Ascend to Camps I and II for acclimatization
  • Final preparation for the ascentSummit attempt on March 18, 2024
  • Summit attempt on March 18, 2024

    • Days 19-21 (March 19-21, 2024): Descent and Departure

  • Descend to Everest Base Camp and Lukla
  • Fly back to Kathmandu on March 21, 2024
  • Farewell dinner and cultural exchange in Kathmandu
