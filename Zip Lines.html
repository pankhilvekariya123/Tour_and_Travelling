__Zip Lines__

Many of the ziplines mentioned below claim to be the longest zipline in the world. But this exhilarating zipline, in Ras al Khaimah, has been officially recognized by the Guinness World Records as being the longest one! It is a record-breaking 1.76 miles long (2.83kms). It is also the fastest zipline in the world, reaching speeds of up to 100 miles per hour (160km per hr). The other really cool thing about it is you get to strike your superman pose, as the zipline has you suspended face down, soaring at astonishing heights.

__Zipline in Ras al Khaimah__

The platform you launch from is also out-of-this-world. It is a free-standing platform with a see-through bottom, suspended from the mountains by mere cables. I can't think of a more unique way to experience the Eifel Tower and surrounds, than by launching from a 377-foot platform located on the tower itself!
